The Sponsor's Showcase

About the Event

Get ready for the Sponsors Showcase, an exciting ADZAP-style event where you must sell the weirdest everyday objects with creativity, humor, and confidence! Pick a slip, prepare your pitch, and convince the audience like your life depends on it.

Rules & Guidelines

  • Preparation Time: 4-5 mins| Pitch Time: 2-3 mins.
  • Team members should not exceed 3.
  • Language: English or Tamil.
  • Judging: Creativity, Convincing power, Humor, Teamwork.
  • No Vulgar/offensive content.
  • Usuage of External props is not allowed.
